Anson's Capstone Project: Map of The Harley School

Introduction

Project Log

This is the log of my progress in this project, where I listed the steps that leads to the completion of this project

The idea of this project is suggested by Dr.Dobson. As a relatively new teacher in the Harley School, he realized that one of the difficulty for those new to Harley is recognizing the different names/symbols of a room, as one room can be refered to in different names including a conbination of letters and numbers

Therefore, we decided to make an interactive map, where its primary function is to represent and visualize Harley in a Google map, and a location can be searched and located with various different tags, including associated displine, teacher, function, etc.

To develop a map embedded in a website, a level of understanding of the three website developing languages (HTML, CSS and JavaScript) is neccessary. I started to learn and progress through the three language with W3Schools and making an simple Testing site.
